Peanut Butter Noodles

Peanut butter ban mian noodles are a popular Chinese dish that combines chewy handmade wheat noodles with a savory peanut butter sauce. The sauce is typically made by blending together peanut butter, soy sauce, chili oil, and others, creating a flavorful and slightly spicy dressing.

The dish is often topped with vegetables such as bean sprouts, scallions, and spinach, and sometimes includes protein such as meat or tofu. The combination of chewy noodles and rich, nutty sauce make for a delicious and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Soy Sauce
  • Neutral Oil (ex. canola, sesame)
  • Chili Oil
  • MSG
  • Peanut Butter
  • Noodles of your choice
  • Scallions

Steps

  1. Bring pot of water to boil. Cook noodles.
  2. Combine soy sauce, oils, MSG, and peanut butter in a separate bowl.
  3. Drain noodles and put in bowl. Mix.
  4. Top with scallions.
