Lemonade

Lemonade is a refreshing and tangy drink made by mixing lemon juice, water, and sugar. It is a popular beverage during the summer months, and can be served cold or over ice.

Lemonade can be made with freshly squeezed lemons or with store-bought lemon juice, and can be customized with additional ingredients such as mint leaves or berries for added flavor.

It is a simple yet classic drink that is enjoyed by people of all ages and can be found in many cafes and restaurants around the world.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 9 lemons
  • 7 cups ice water

Steps

  1. Combine sugar and 1 cup water. Stir to dissolve, until boiling.
  2. Squeeze lemons into measuring cup. Discard seeds.
  3. Pour 7 cups water into pitcher. Stir in lemon juice and pulp, then simple syrup to taste.
